The Essence of Elegance

Our Story

Uncompromising Luxury and Specialized Beauty in Lahore since 2011

How We Began

Redefining Beauty Standards

Kashi Beauty Salon was founded with a single, clear objective: to bring high-end, luxury beauty and makeup artistry to Lahore. We didn't want to be another standard parlor; we aimed to create a luxurious sanctuary where beauty, hygiene, and wellness are treated as fine art.

For over a decade, we have been Johar Town’s premier destination for brides seeking flawless, high-definition makeup, and women looking for advanced hair transformation and dermatologically safe skin therapies.

Our Vision

To remain the leading standard of luxury makeup styling and bridal elegance in Pakistan, expanding techniques to deliver global beauty trends locally.

Our Mission

To provide a sanitized, premium environment using genuine international products, delivering expert makeovers that highlight each client's unique radiance.

Our Milestones

Salon Timeline

2011

The Beginning

Kashi Beauty Salon opened its doors in Johar Town, Lahore, with three dedicated beauticians and a promise of hygiene and premium quality.

2015

Bridal Studio Launch

Recognizing the need for premium bridal care, we launched our exclusive Bridal Preparation Studio, recruiting international makeup trainers.

2020

Advanced Hair & Skin Suites

We expanded our facilities to include private Hydra Facial treatment rooms and custom hair color balayage stations, partnering with premium global brands.

2026

Over 10,000 Transformations

Celebrating a decade and a half of beauty, we continue to serve Lahore's style-conscious community with unparalleled luxury and elegance.

Beauty Philosophy

"Timeless, Elegance, Elevated"

We believe that makeup should not change who you are, but elevate the natural grace you possess. By custom blending foundations, assessing facial symmetries, and respecting skin wellness, our looks remain fresh, photographs beautifully, and leaves you feeling absolutely radiant.
